About Studio

The Master Artistry

Lashed by LC is a Jamaica based lash business founded in 2015 by Latoya Clarke who is an established master lash artist with years of experience. Our main goal at Lashed by LC is to provide quality luxury lash and waxing services along with excellent customer service. We want all our clients to leave feeling like their best self. We also provide detailed training and lash extension products to professionals looking to join the lash industry.
